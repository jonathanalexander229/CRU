Shaking Beef
$16
Ingredients:
Beef tips, large cubes 5 oz
Shaking beef marinade 3 oz
Arugula, dressed in EVOO 0.25 oz
White sesame seeds garnish
Method:
Preheat cast iron skillet to near smoke point. Add oil, add beef cubes. Hard sear first side, then shake pan vigorously to sear other sides and cook to medium. Glaze with spicy lemon sauce, reduce. Place dressed arugula down center of plate, place seared beef on arugula, garnish with sesame seeds.
