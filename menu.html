Edamame Hummus

Yield Recipe

Base Hummus

  • Chickpeas, drained 60 oz
  • Lemon juice 1 cup
  • Tahini 1 cup
  • Garlic cloves, chopped 3 ea
  • Olive oil blend 1.25 cup
  • Ground cumin 2 tsp
  • Water 0.5 cup

Edamame Addition

  • Shelled edamame, thawed 2 lbs
  • Water 1.5 cups
  • Base hummus 8 cups
  • Lemon juice as needed
  • S&P as needed

Method

Place drained garbanzo beans in robot coupe. Add water, garlic, tahini & lemon until smooth paste forms. Add olive oil slowly, drizzle oil into hummus with robot. Remove hummus from robot coupe. Process shelled edamame with 1.5 cups water until smooth paste forms. Add to bowl with hummus, stir with a spatula to incorporate. Season with salt & pepper and additional lemon juice to taste.

Shaking Beef

Earthenware Rectangle Plate

Ingredients

  • Beef tips, large cubes 5 oz
  • Shaking beef marinade 3 oz
  • Arugula, dressed in EVOO 0.25 oz
  • White sesame seeds garnish

Method

Preheat a cast iron skillet to near smoke point. Add small amount of oil, add beef cubes. Hard sear the first side of beef, then shake the pan vigorously to sear other sides of beef and cook to medium. Glaze beef with spicy lemon sauce, reduce. Place dressed arugula down center of plate, place seared beef on arugula, garnish with sesame seeds.

Cast Iron Seared Shrimp

12" Round Bowl

Main Components

  • Red Argentine Shrimp (16-20) 0.3 lb
  • Spinach Parmesan Risotto 3 oz
  • Microgreens garnish

Sauce

  • Yellow cherry tomatoes, halved 4 ea
  • Garlic, minced 0.5 tsp
  • Lemon juice 0.5 oz
  • White wine 1 oz
  • S&P 0.13 TT
  • Butter, softened 0.5 oz
  • Basil, chiffonade 0.13 leaf

Method

Sear shrimp in a CAST IRON skillet until seared, rotate begin making sauce. Plate risotto in the center of a coupe bowl and arrange scallops around risotto. Spoon sauce on and around the shrimp, garnish with microgreens. To make sauce: Heat olive oil in a sauté pan, add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds, add yellow tomatoes, lemon juice, white wine, salt and pepper. Reduce to sauce consistency. Add butter, and incorporate.

Pan Seared Salmon

12" Round Bowl

Main Components

  • Salmon Filet 7 oz
  • Haricot vert, blanched 9 pc
  • Campari tomatoes, roasted on vine 2 ea

Lemon Dill Butter Sauce

  • White wine 1 oz
  • Lemon juice 0.5 oz
  • Lemon wheels, sliced thin 1 ea
  • Butter, softened 1 oz
  • Dill, chopped 0.13 tt
  • S&p 0.13 tt

Method

Season the salmon with salt and pepper. In a hot pan sear until golden brown. Remove from the pan and place on a sizzle plate and finish in the oven. Heat green beans with small amount of butter and S&P. Make the sauce in the fish pan by adding the white wine, lemon juice, lemon wheel, dill & s&p, reduce and incorporate the butter. Plate green beans in center of bowl, place salmon on top of the beans, pour sauce over the fish. Garnish the salmon with the lemon wheel, roasted tomatoes & microgreens.
